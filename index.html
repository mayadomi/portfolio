About me:


Admittedly something of a cultural chameleon, I grew up in Geneva, Switzerland to international parents and eventually made my way to Perth, Australia and currently now in Brisbane. I started out studying veterinary science before swapping to Molecular Biology, after which I still didn't really know 'what to do with my life'.


At this point I stumbled into the field of geographic information science after remembering how much I enjoyed a project I did in school on urban heat islands, and felt like I'd been given a secret map to so much of life's mysteries. I came to appreciate how special spatial is, and depths of meaning a spatial perspective can provide in so many aspects of life.


After a period of time navigating this space, the yearn for learning and discovering new horizons relit a fire for change and I was lucky enough to succeed in getting a spot in the She Codes Plus program. It has been a transformational experience and I'm beyond excited to see where my path takes me next.


When I'm not tinkering with anything at a computer, you'll likely find me out riding my bicycle exploring and adventuring, helping others learn to ride, or enjoying a good coffee and indulging my sweet tooth somewhere.

Projects

Racing to Break the Cycle of Domestic Violence

A React & DRF Crowdfunding Project

This project was completed as part of our first full stack project. It was built using Django Rest Framework (DRF) for the backend and React JS for the frontend. The concept of the site is to help fundraise for domestic violence charities by empowering women to cycle and participate in this annual cycling event run by LifeCycle Cycling Club. The deployed app can be found here, and the backend repo is here and the frontend repo is here.
