About

Hi, I'm Maya

I'm a versatile and driven individual who thrives in the creative spaces between spatial developer, designer and cartographer. I have an insatiable love for learning and sharing, and constantly seek out new opportunities to expand my knowledge, skill-sets and connect with others over creative endeavours. A solution-focussed innovative mindset is always front of mind, and I strive to seamlessly blend creativity and technical expertise in all my work.


When I'm not tinkering with anything at a computer, you'll likely find me out riding my bicycle exploring and adventuring, helping others learn to ride, or enjoying a good coffee brew somewhere.
